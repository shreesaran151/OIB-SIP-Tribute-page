Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of the greatest scientists and leaders of India. He inspired millions with his vision, simplicity, and dedication to the nation.

Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a key role in India's space and defense programs. His words continue to motivate students to dream big and work hard.

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."
